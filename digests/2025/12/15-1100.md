### [If AI replaces workers, should it also pay taxes?](https://english.elpais.com/technology/2025-11-30/if-ai-replaces-workers-should-it-also-pay-taxes.html) • 131pts 208c
<!-- i18n:zh -->### 如果AI取代工人，它也应该交税吗？<!-- /i18n -->

[HN discussion](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46268709)

TLDR: Labor income is 85% of US tax revenue. As AI replaces workers, that revenue disappears. Bill Gates and Nobel laureate Edmund Phelps suggest taxing robots at the same rate as humans. Critics say it's impossible to define AI precisely and would hurt competitiveness.
<!-- i18n:zh -->TLDR: 劳动收入占美国税收的85%。AI取代工人后，这些税收就消失了。比尔·盖茨和诺贝尔奖得主Edmund Phelps建议按人类税率给机器人征税。批评者说AI无法精确定义，会损害竞争力。<!-- /i18n -->

Take: The real issue isn't whether to tax AI, it's that capital gains are taxed lower than labor. Tax the owners who profit from automation, not the automation itself. The robot didn't choose to replace you.
<!-- i18n:zh -->Take: 真正的问题不是是否给AI征税，而是资本利得税比劳动税低。给从自动化中获利的所有者征税，而不是自动化本身。机器人没有选择取代你。<!-- /i18n -->
