### [Home Depot GitHub token exposed for a year](https://techcrunch.com/2025/12/12/home-depot-exposed-access-to-internal-systems-for-a-year-says-researcher/) • 201pts 119c
[HN discussion](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46247000)
TLDR: 安全研究员 Ben Zimmermann 发现一个 Home Depot 员工的 GitHub token 自 2024 年初以来一直公开，授予访问数百个私有仓库和云基础设施的权限，包括订单履行系统。Home Depot 无视他，直到 TechCrunch 来敲门。
Take: 真正的恐怖不是他们让一个 token 暴露了一年。而是他们不知道是否有其他人使用过它。安心睡觉吧，知道你的订单历史可能没事。
Comment: "Last week I accidentally exposed my OpenAI, Anthropic, and Gemini keys. Within seconds I got an email from Anthropic and they have already disabled my keys. Neither OpenAI nor Google alerted me in anyway." -dnw

### [String theory inspires a brilliant, baffling new math proof](https://www.quantamagazine.org/string-theory-inspires-a-brilliant-baffling-new-math-proof-20251212/) • 115pts 108c
[HN discussion](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46245622)
TLDR: Maxim Kontsevich 和团队证明了四维多项式方程不能被简单参数化，使用的技术来自弦理论，代数几何学家实际上称之为"黑魔法"。全世界的数学家正在组建阅读小组来理解它。
Take: 弦理论可能永远不会预测一个物理测量，但至少它让纯数学家保持谦逊和困惑。这几乎值得几十年的炒作。
Comment: "That's arguably what String Theory is good for, producing interesting, entertaining, and possibly even useful math." -EA-3167

### [Security issues with electronic invoices](https://invoice.secvuln.info/) • 75pts 42c
[HN discussion](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46248470)
TLDR: 欧盟的标准化电子发票生态系统存在广泛的 XML 外部实体（XXE）漏洞。研究人员在多个发票工具中发现了 Java/Saxon XML 解析器的不安全默认值，使文件泄露和未经授权的系统访问成为可能。
Take: XML：不断给安全研究人员提供事情做的礼物。三十年过去了，我们仍在发现没有人正确配置他们的解析器。
Comment: "This talk seems set out to prove that 'XML is Bad'. Yes XML-DSig isn't great with XPaths, but most of these attack vectors has been known for 10 years." -tnorgaard
